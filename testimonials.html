Before, During, and After

A simple way to share the change - what life was like before Metameta, the support received, and where alumni are today.

Before Metameta

  • Unstable housing or school attendance
  • Limited access to daily meals
  • Little emotional or academic support

At Metameta

  • Safe shelter and consistent routines
  • Learning support and mentorship
  • Healthy meals and wellness care

Currently

  • Continuing education or employment
  • Strong self-confidence and goals
  • Giving back to their communities
