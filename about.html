Our Story

Metameta Children's Home began with a simple vision — to provide a safe place for children who had nowhere else to go. What started as a small initiative has grown into a family that continues to transform lives every day.

Over the years, we have supported dozens of children, helping them transition from vulnerability to independence through education, care, and mentorship.
