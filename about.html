Our Story

Metameta Children's Home began with a simple yet powerful vision — to provide a safe haven for children who had nowhere else to go. What started as a small initiative in 2004 with just 5 children has blossomed into a loving family that continues to transform lives every single day.

Over the past two decades, we have supported over 48 children, helping them transition from vulnerability to independence through quality education, compassionate care, and dedicated mentorship.

💙 "Every child who walks through our doors becomes family. We don't just provide shelter — we provide hope, love, and a future."

Today, Metameta stands as a beacon of hope in our community, ensuring that every child receives not just the basics of food and shelter, but also the emotional support, educational opportunities, and life skills needed to break the cycle of poverty and build a brighter tomorrow.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide a loving, safe, and nurturing environment where every child can thrive, receive quality education, and develop essential life skills for a successful future.

👁️ Our Vision

A world where every child has a home, an education, and the opportunity to reach their full potential regardless of their background.

💖 Our Values

Compassion • Integrity • Excellence • Hope • Family. These values guide everything we do at Metameta Children's Home.

Our Journey Through the Years

🏠 2004

Metameta Children's Home was founded with just 5 children and a dream.

📚 2010

Expanded facilities to support more children and launched education programs.

💻 2018

Introduced digital skills and computer literacy programs.

🎓 2024
