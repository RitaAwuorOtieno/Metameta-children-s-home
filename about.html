About Metameta Children's Home

Who We Are

Founded in 2004, Metameta Children's Home is a registered organization committed to providing care and protection to vulnerable children. For over two decades, we have worked to create a safe and nurturing environment where children can grow, learn, and thrive.

Our Mission

To provide holistic care including shelter, education, healthcare, emotional support, and spiritual guidance — empowering children to build independent and meaningful futures.

Our Vision

A society where every child is valued, protected, and given equal opportunity to reach their full potential.

A Message From The Director

“Since 2004, our goal has been simple — to give children not only a place to stay, but a place to belong. Every child deserves love, dignity, and hope. We are grateful for every supporter who walks this journey with us.”
