FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our work

How can I donate?

You can donate online through our secure donation page, bank transfer, or mobile money. Visit our Support page for more details.

Can I visit the children's home?

Yes! We welcome visitors by appointment. Please contact us to schedule a visit and learn more about our work.

How can I volunteer?

We have various volunteering opportunities including teaching, mentoring, and event support. Fill out the form above and select "Volunteer Opportunity".

Do you accept corporate partnerships?

Absolutely! We partner with companies for sponsorships, employee volunteering, and corporate donations. Contact us to discuss partnership opportunities.

Can I sponsor a child?

Yes! Child sponsorship provides education, meals, and care. Visit our Programs page to learn about sponsorship options.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, we are a registered charity. All donations are tax-deductible. We provide receipts for all contributions.
