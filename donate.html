FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about donating

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! We are a registered charity. All donations are tax-deductible and we provide official receipts for every contribution.

How is my donation used?

100% of donations go directly to children's care, education, food, shelter, and medical needs. We maintain complete transparency.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Absolutely! You can set up monthly donations via M-Pesa or bank transfer. Contact us to arrange recurring support.

Can I donate from outside Kenya?

Yes! International donors can use bank transfer, PayPal, or Western Union. Contact us for assistance with international giving.

Will I receive updates on my impact?

Yes! All donors receive quarterly updates, photos, and impact reports showing how your support is changing lives.

Can I sponsor a specific child?

Yes! Our child sponsorship program allows you to support an individual child's education and care. Contact us to learn more.
