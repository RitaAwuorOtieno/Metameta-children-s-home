Our Programs

Supporting every child through care, education, and opportunity

Programs at Metameta

Each program is designed to meet real needs — from daily care to long-term empowerment. We believe every child deserves support in every stage of life.

🏠 Shelter & Care

We provide a safe and loving home environment where children feel secure, valued, and part of a family.

🥗 Feeding Program

Nutritious meals are provided daily to ensure healthy growth and development.

📚 Education Support

We support school attendance, provide learning materials, and encourage academic excellence.

💻 Computer Training

Children are introduced to digital skills to prepare them for the modern world.

❤️ Counseling & Mentorship

Emotional support and mentorship help children heal, grow, and build confidence.

⚽ Talent Development

Sports and creative activities help children discover and develop their talents.

Why These Programs Matter

Every program plays a role in shaping a child’s future — from basic needs to long-term independence.
