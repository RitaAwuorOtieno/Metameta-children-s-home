Our Programs

Supporting every child through care, education, and opportunity

What We Do

Our programs are designed to nurture, educate, and empower every child for a brighter and independent future.

🏠 Shelter & Care

A safe, loving environment where children feel protected, valued, and at home.

🥗 Feeding Program

Daily nutritious meals that support healthy growth and well-being.

📚 Education

School support, materials, and mentorship to encourage academic success.

💻 Digital Skills

Basic ICT training to prepare children for modern opportunities.

❤️ Counseling

Emotional healing and mentorship to build confidence and resilience.

⚽ Talent Development

Sports and creative programs that unlock each child’s potential.

Beyond Basic Needs

At Metameta, we don’t just provide care — we build futures. Every program is designed to give children the skills, confidence, and opportunities they need to succeed in life.
