Our Programs

Four core programs help children feel safe, nourished, and prepared for the future.

Programs at Metameta

Each program is designed to meet a real need - from early childhood care to digital skills. Together, they build confidence, healthy routines, and opportunity.

01

Daycare Program

A warm, structured space for young children to grow and learn during the day.

  • Safe supervision and play-based learning
  • Early childhood routines and social skills
  • Healthy snacks and nap time support
🥗

Daily nourishment

Meals that fuel growth, focus, and energy.

02

Feeding Program

Daily meals that keep children strong, energized, and ready to learn.

  • Nutritious breakfasts and hot lunches
  • Seasonal fruits and vegetables
  • Food security support for vulnerable families
📚

Stories and study time

Books, mentoring, and calm spaces to read.

03

Library & Reading Program

Books, story time, and literacy support for every reading level.

  • Access to storybooks and textbooks
  • Reading circles and homework help
  • Quiet study space for focused learning
💻

Digital skills

Confidence with tools needed for modern learning.

04

Computer Program

Digital literacy and practical skills for school, work, and daily life.

  • Typing, internet research, and basic software
  • Guided projects and educational tools
  • Mentorship for future career paths

Why These Programs Matter

We focus on the essentials: safety, nutrition, literacy, and technology. These programs support children today while preparing them for independent futures.
